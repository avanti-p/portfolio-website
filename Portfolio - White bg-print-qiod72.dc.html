THE CHALLENGE

Millions of individuals who qualify for public benefits programs — food assistance, healthcare, housing support, childcare subsidies, and more — never access them. The barriers aren't just logistical; they're deeply rooted in complexity, language, and systemic inaccessibility.

The challenge was to design an experience that meets people where they are — one that feels human, trustworthy, and empowering rather than bureaucratic and overwhelming.
