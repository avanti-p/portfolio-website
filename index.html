THE CHALLENGE

Public benefits can change someone's life—but figuring out what you qualify for often feels like solving a puzzle with missing pieces.

Rules vary by state. Eligibility depends on dozens of factors. Case managers are overwhelmed, and people who qualify often never apply.

Our challenge wasn't just to build another screening tool.

It was to build something people could actually trust.

MY ROLE

I was the product designer responsible for turning policy (for SNAP, WIC, TANF, LIHEAP, and CAPS) into product.

From discovery through launch, I partnered with leadership, engineers, and on-staff policy experts to define what we should build, why it mattered, and how to make it approachable for the people who needed it most.

03

FEATURED COMPONENTS

A visual walkthrough of the pieces I designed. Drop in screens and process shots to let the work speak for itself.

Eligibility Screening Tool
Making complicated questions feel simple.
Reduced cognitive load Plain language Multi-state policy

The engine weighs hundreds of policy rules behind the scenes—but users never have to. I guided people one step at a time with plain language, progressive disclosure, and conversational flows.

Personalized Results
People don't need more data—they need clarity.
Est. monthly benefit Personalized picks Real-time logic

Instead of drowning people in program details, the results view leads with estimated monthly benefit value and a clear, approachable summary—so impact lands in seconds and the next step feels obvious.

Support Plans
From "You're eligible" to "Here's exactly what to do."
Personalized steps Always available Accessible reading

One of my favorite features. Support Plans turn eligibility results into personalized action steps in clear, easy-to-read language—a practical checklist tailored to each person's situation, not a maze of government sites.

Admin Dashboard
Less time searching—more time supporting.
Org-wide reporting Role-based views Progress tracking

Case managers need answers, not another dashboard. This view puts client progress, eligibility outcomes, and platform activity in one place—supporting faster decisions and more informed conversations.

Client Dashboard
One place to pick up right where you left off.
Personalized home Saved plans Progress tracking

Applying for benefits rarely happens in one sitting. A personalized dashboard keeps results, Support Plans, and recommended resources together—so people can return with confidence and stay organized from start to finish.

04

THE OUTCOMES

This project wasn't just about launching software.

It helped organizations identify more eligible clients, simplify case management, and connect more people with essential resources.

Just as importantly, it showed how thoughtful product design can transform complicated policy into something people actually understand.
