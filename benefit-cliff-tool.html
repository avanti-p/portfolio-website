THE CHALLENGE

Accepting a better-paying job should be simple, but for millions of Americans it isn't.

A small increase in income can reduce or eliminate multiple benefits at once, causing what's known as a "benefits cliff". This often leaves workers & job-seekers unsure whether a job or raise actually makes them better off financially. Case managers often spend hours manually comparing employment scenarios, while clients are forced to make important employment decisions with incomplete information.

My goal was to design a tool that helped people confidently answer: "What happens to my benefits if I earn more?"

1 in 3
employers report that workers have declined jobs, raises, or additional hours because they feared losing public benefits.

FEATURED COMPONENTS

Five pieces of the platform, each framed by the problem it solves.

Eligibility Screening Tool
Reduced cognitive load Plain language Multi-state policy
THE PROBLEM
Existing screening tools are long, confusing, and filled with government terminology. Organizations also spend significant staff time manually determining eligibility, leading to inconsistent results and slower client support.
THE SOLUTION
A guided eligibility experience that translates >95 pages of government forms, across multiple benefits, into a simple and quick screener. People see one plain-language question at a time, revealed progressively so nothing feels overwhelming, reducing the burden for multiple on both clients and case managers.
Personalized Results
Est. monthly benefit Information hierarchy Real-time logic
THE PROBLEM
Many eligibility tools stop after telling someone they qualify, leaving users unsure of the actual value of applying or which programs deserve their attention first.
THE SOLUTION
A personalized results page, powered by BestFit's eligibility and benefit-estimation algorithm, is generated in real-time. Users see their potential monthly benefit amount based on their actual answers, helping them better understand the financial impact of available programs.
Support Plans
Personalized steps Always available Accessible reading
THE PROBLEM
Even after learning they qualify, people are left to untangle applications across a maze of separate agency sites and documents needed.
THE SOLUTION
Support Plans translate eligibility results into a plain-language task list — the exact next steps, in order, all in one place. Case managers and clients can asynchronously update tasks, allowing organizations to support more people without increasing staff workload.
Client Dashboard
Personalized home Saved plans Progress tracking
THE PROBLEM
Benefits applications rarely finish in one sitting, and people lose momentum — and trust — when they can't find where they left off.
THE SOLUTION
A centralized dashboard where users can revisit their eligibility results, access their Support Plan, track progress, and quickly return to resources whenever they're ready to continue.
Admin Dashboard
Org-wide reporting Role-based views Progress tracking
THE PROBLEM
Organizations lacked visibility into how clients were using the platform and had limited insight into outcomes, making it difficult to measure impact, identify trends, or support grant reporting.
THE SOLUTION
The admin dashboard gives staff access to screening activity, eligibility trends, client engagement, and program insights, helping organizations make data-informed decisions and demonstrate community impact.
